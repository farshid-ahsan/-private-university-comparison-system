Description

BRAC University (BRACU) is a private University in Bangladesh. It was founded as a branch of the BRAC organization by Fazle Hasan Abed in 2001 under the Private University Act, The university ranked 1st among the private universities and 3rd among all the universities in research output on the QS Asian University Ranking 2018 Bangladesh

BRAC UNIVERSITY RANKING AND REVIEW

BRAC University is one of the top ranking private universities in Bangladesh. The campus of BRACU is located at Mohakhali, Dhaka. The university is also building its permanent campus located at Pragati Sarani, Dhaka. The university is supposed to shift to its permanent campus by 2018. Fazle Hasan Abed, a Bangladeshi social worker is the founder of BRAC University. Professor Syed Saad Andaleeb is working as Vice-Chancellor of BRAC University. BRAC University Ranking

BRAC UNIVERSITY ADMISSION INFORMATION

BRAC University (BRACU) always pays close attention to convenient approach in terms of Admission Procedure. The entire process of admission goes through an intensely selective method where applicants are asked to follow each phase precisely within the given instructions. As the admission test is a highly competitive one, applicants require ensuring satisfactory results in the examination in order to secure their seats. Academic Requirements for Admissions in BRAC University Minimum GPA of 3.50 in SSC/equivalent and HSC/equivalent separately (including additional subject).
