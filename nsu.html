Description

North South University (NSU) is the first private university in Bangladesh, was established by a group of philanthropists, industrialists, bureaucrats and academics. The government of Bangladesh approved to establishment of North South University in 1992 under Private University Act-1992. The university was formally inaugurated on 10 February, 1993. The honorable President of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh is the Chancellor of North South University.

North South UNIVERSITY ADMISSION INFORMATION

Minimum requirements for Programs: For all programs of School of Engineering & Physical Sciences, candidates must have Math or Physics, with minimum B grade in HSC or C grade in A-levels to be eligible to apply. For Biochemistry and Microbiology, applicants must have Biology and Chemistry at SSC/HSC or O/A-levels to be eligible to apply. For BPharm Professional, applicants must have Biology & Chemistry, with, minimum B grade in HSC and C grade in A-levels and should have done Physics in HSC/A-levels to be eligible to apply. Minimum Qualifications for admission test: Academic Qualifications: Combined GPA of 8.0 in both SSC and HSC with minimum GPA 3.5 in each OR GCE O-Levels and A-Levels: O-Levels in five subjects with Average Grade Point of 2.5 or above and A-levels in two subjects with Average Grade Point of 2.0 or above. (scale A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2 & E=1) OR US High School Diploma, IB Diploma Programme (IB-DP) or equivalent Academic qualification other than the above need to contact Admissions Office to check the eligibility Foreign applicants should send their academic transcripts and name of two referees for initial screening to admissions@northsouth.edu. After verification, Admissions Office will contact with them about their eligibility for admission. Acceptable scores in NSU’s admission test. Admission test will be waived for foreign eligible candidates and local candidates with i. a minimum score of 1500 (OLD) or 1000 (New) in SAT AND ii. 500 (CBT 213/IBT 79) in TOEFL OR Minimum 5.5 in IELTS

North South UNIVERSITY SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID

Financial aid for the wards of freedom fighters (WoFF) of liberation war (Quota based waiver). Financial aid based on previous academic attainment and admission test result (Merit based tuition waiver). Financial aid based on high academic achievement at NSU (Merit based tuition waiver). Financial aid based on financial NEED of a student (based on humanitarian ground). Financial aid based on Need and Merit of a student (Merit-Need based waiver). Financial aid based on siblings study together at NSU. Financial aid in the form of student employment at NSU (Work-Study based waiver).

Host Nations: A Refugee Simulation

South Asian Institute of Policy and Governance (SIPG) at North South University, Dhaka has hosted the 2019 NASPAA-Batten Student Simulation Competition on 23rd February 2019. NASPAA is global accreditor in public service education. It is the membership organization of graduate education programs in public policy, public affairs, public administration, and public & nonprofit management. Its over 300 members - located across the U.S. and in 24 countries around the globe - award MPA, MPP, MPAff, and similar degrees. NASPAA is the recognized global accreditor of master’s degree programs in these fields.

North South University visits Northwestern Polytechnical University, China

Recently Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) located in Xi’an, China, invited North South University (NSU) to visit NPU from October 13th - 15th, 2018. The NSU delegation consisted of honorable Vice Chancellor of NSU, Dr. Atique Islam and Dr. Shahnewaz Siddique, Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at NSU. During this visit NSU attended a number of official programs of NPU including NPU’s 80th anniversary celebrations and the 2018 Global Partner Week Presidents Forum.

Training for Economic Relations Division (ERD) officials

South Asian Institute of Policy and Governance (SIPG) of North South University has partnered with Economic Relations Division of Ministry of Finance to extend the academic support in a two day long training session for the officials of Economic Relations Division (ERD) under Ministry of Finance, titled Managing Change for Innovation held on 19-20th September, 2018 at the premise of ERD. The aim of such training is to equip the officials with up to date skills and knowledge on Innovation and change management to manage the forthcoming challenges after elevating to the middle income country. The faculty members of SIPG conducted this training program. The sessions of the training combined with the theoretical aspects of Innovation and Change Management as well as hands on application of the theory through various tools. The Economic Relations Division is the focal point of the government for interfacing development partners and coordinates all external assistance inflows into the country
